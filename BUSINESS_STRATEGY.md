## 1. Strategic positioning

| I sell | I do **not** sell |
|---|---|
| Senior engineering judgment **amplified by AI** | "An AI that writes your app" |
| Incremental, low-risk legacy modernization | Big-bang rewrites |
| Reconstructed, living knowledge bases | One-off stale documentation |
| A repeatable, governed method (AIAD) | Unmanaged code generation / hype |

**The wedge:** the market is flooded with two extremes — cheap "AI will replace
developers" promises, and conservative consultancies that ignore AI. I sit in the
profitable gap: **a 30-year senior engineer who uses AI as a force multiplier under
strict engineering discipline.** That is rare, credible, and high-value.

### 1.2 Unique value proposition (UVP)

> *"30 years of enterprise engineering, multiplied by AI. I migrate your legacy
> systems to Java, Go or Rust — and rebuild the knowledge your business has lost —
> incrementally, safely, and 3–5× faster than a traditional rewrite."*

## 2. Service catalog (productized offers)

Productizing turns "freelance hours" into **outcomes with a price tag** — easier to
sell, easier to scope, higher margin.

### Offer 1 — Legacy → Modern Migration (Java / Go / Rust) with AIAD
Convert legacy code (C/C++, old Java, PL/SQL-heavy logic, Node.js, monoliths) into
modern, maintainable Java, Go or Rust services — incrementally, with old and new
running side by side. Production never stops.
- **Outcome:** a modern, tested, documented module/service replacing a legacy one.
- **Deliverables:** target architecture, migrated code, integration tests, proof-of-execution reports, knowledge artifacts.

### Offer 2 — Knowledge Base Reconstruction
Recover the business logic and architecture trapped inside the code. AI explores the
legacy system; I curate and validate (the *Humanizer Phase*) into a living,
continuously-updated source of truth.
- **Outcome:** a reconstructed, accurate, maintainable knowledge base + diagrams.
- **Deliverables:** domain model, business-rule catalog, dependency maps, decision records.

### Offer 3 — AIAD Enablement / Fractional AI-Engineering Lead
Install the AIAD method inside the client's own team: tooling, guardrails, review
discipline, and the Humanizer Phase — so they keep moving safely after I leave.
- **Outcome:** a team that ships faster with AI **without** creating technical debt.
- **Deliverables:** AIAD playbook, prompt/spec templates, review gates, training, coaching.

### Entry product — Legacy Modernization Audit (paid, fixed fee)
A 5–10 day fixed-price assessment: AI-assisted scan of the codebase, risk map,
modernization roadmap, and a quantified business case. **This is the foot-in-the-door
that converts into Offers 1–3.**

## 3. Pricing & engagement models

> Numbers are **starting reference points** for a senior, English-speaking EU
> freelancer selling outcomes (not commodity dev hours). Tune per market & client.

| Model | Reference price | When to use |
|---|---|---|
| **Modernization Audit** (fixed) | €4,500 – €9,000 | First engagement, low-commitment entry |
| **Day rate** (T&M) | €750 – €1,100 / day | Ongoing migration / embedded work |
| **Migration sprint** (fixed) | €12,000 – €30,000 / module | Well-scoped legacy module → modern |
| **Knowledge reconstruction** (fixed) | €8,000 – €20,000 | Bounded subsystem |
| **AIAD retainer** (monthly) | €4,000 – €9,000 / mo | Fractional lead / enablement |
| **Value share** (optional) | base + % of savings | When you can measure cost/perf gains |

**Pricing principles**
1. Always anchor on **outcome and risk avoided**, not hours.
2. Lead with the **fixed-fee Audit**; price the audit so it's an easy "yes".
3. Offer 3 tiers on big proposals (Good / Better / Best) — most pick the middle.
4. Raise rates ~10–15% every 2–3 closed clients until you hit resistance.

## 4. Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

**Primary ICP**
- Mid-to-large companies (50–5,000 staff) with **business-critical legacy systems**.
- Verticals where I have domain trust: **finance / banking / asset management,
  aviation & travel, insurance, public sector / govtech, logistics**.
- Trigger events: senior devs retiring, failed/feared rewrite, compliance/audit
  pressure, cloud migration, performance crisis, M&A integration.

**Buyer personas:** CTO, VP/Director of Engineering, Head of Architecture,
Modernization/Cloud program lead, IT Director in regulated industries.

**Why they buy from me:** the cost of *getting modernization wrong* (downtime, lost
business rules, blown budgets) is enormous — they pay a premium for a senior who
de-risks it.

## 5. Where to prospect international customers

Ranked by expected ROI for a solo senior consultant. Work the top of the list first.

### Tier 1 — Highest leverage (start here)
1. **LinkedIn (outbound + content).** Your #1 channel for B2B.
   - Optimize the profile around the UVP ("Legacy → Java/Go/Rust with AIAD").
   - Post 2–3×/week: migration war stories, the 7min→5s Rust case, AIAD principles.
   - Use **Sales Navigator** to target CTOs/Eng Directors in ICP verticals (DACH,
     UK, Benelux, Nordics, France, North America) and send personalized outreach.
2. **Warm network reactivation.** 30 years = a large network. Email/LinkedIn every
   former client, colleague, and manager (Atos, Ausy, CACEIS, GlobalJet, KBL…).
   Referrals from people who saw your work close the fastest.
3. **Targeted cold email** to ICP companies with a specific trigger + the Audit offer.

- **Toptal** (top 3% network; premium clients, US-heavy) — apply, it's a credibility badge.
- **Malt** (strong in France, DACH, Spain, Benelux — ideal for an EU freelancer).

- **freelancermap** (DACH IT contracting), **Comatch/COMATCH→ now BCG Expand**, **Expert360** (consulting-style gigs).
- **Upwork** only for premium/long-term — filter hard, it skews price-competitive.

### Tier 3 — Communities & content (inbound over time)
- **Klyrio ([i-smile.one/int](https://i-smile.one/int/))** — your flagship Rust + AIAD product; keep it visible as living proof of the method; case studies and demos sell.
- **Dev communities:** Hacker News (Show HN / case studies), Lobsters, r/rust, r/java,
  r/golang, Rust/Go/Java Discords & Slacks, **Reddit r/ExperiencedDevs**.
- **Conferences & meetups:** Devoxx, JNation, GOTO, QCon, RustConf, GopherCon,
  legacy-modernization / DDD / software-architecture meetups. Speak → instant authority.

### Tier 4 — Partnerships & ecosystems
- **Cloud / SI partner programs** (subcontract overflow modernization work).
- **Boutique consultancies** that need a senior migration specialist on demand.
- **EU innovation/digital-transformation funding** programs (public-sector clients).

### Geographic priority (for an EU, FR/EN consultant)
1. **DACH (Germany, Austria, Switzerland)** — huge legacy estate, strong contracting culture, high rates.
2. **Benelux + Nordics** — English-friendly, mature buyers.
3. **UK + Ireland** — English-native, big financial sector (your domain).
4. **France** — home market, easiest to start (Malt + network).
5. **North America** — via Toptal/A.Team and async remote (mind time zones).
